Paper Reviewer

  1. Upload your paper as a PDF or Markdown (.md) file — format is detected automatically.
  2. PDF files will be converted to Markdown. You will have a chance to review and correct any conversion errors before proceeding.
  3. Citations in the References section should follow one of these formats:
    • [1] Author, A. (2020). Title. In Proceedings of Conference…
    • [LeCun2015] Author et al. Title. NeurIPS 2015.
    • 1. Author, A., & Author, B. (2020). Title. Journal, 10(2), 1–20.
  4. Click Save to write your edits back to the Markdown file.
  5. Click Next → to choose the review configuration and start the paper review.
